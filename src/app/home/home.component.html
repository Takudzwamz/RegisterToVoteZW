About Me

Due to my ambitious and self-driven character, I do stand in many pedestals wearing different hats. I strongly believe in living a principled life, that directly marks a solid positive impact in the world. My desire is to sit at a round table deciding on the betterment of our tomorrow.

I believe that I am a vehicle of change and I am equipped with many different abilities to spread the contagious positivity rooted in me. Being born in a third world country is not a barrier, I was born to make a difference and to make the world a better place. I’m determined to inspire people who are buried on the other side of hope.

My everyday motivation comes from my belief that, if the world can’t offer me on a silver platter, how about I create my own salvation. I believe everyone was destined for greatness, but it only depends on how far you’re willing to go and how much knowledge you’re willing to acquire along the way (Knowledge is Power).
